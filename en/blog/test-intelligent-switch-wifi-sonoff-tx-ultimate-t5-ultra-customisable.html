    Unboxing the Sonoff TX Ultimate T5-86

    Exceptional European-level packaging ðŸ˜€, inside you will find the wall switch in a blister with a protective film on the front of the switch. The first impression that emerges from this product is of exceptional quality, the product is very well finished and the top shell (white) has a satin feel. The LED strip under the switch is very well integrated. ps:domadoo provided me with a T5-2C-86 double switch

    Opening the Sonoff switch TX Ultimate T5-86 is done without forcing, once the 4 screws have been removed you will have access to the printed circuit.

    You can see that Sonoff has not skimped on the number of LEDs, UART access is offered in order to be able to upload customized firmware, on the right is a vibration module.

    Commissioning the Sonoff TX Ultimate T5-86

    Good like the rest of the Wifi range if you want to use this product you will need to download the Ewelink application available on android and Apple Store.

    The Ewelink application is very well done, integrating the modules is hassle-free, if this is your first Sonoff module, start by creating an account. You will see a little further down that it is possible to integrate it into home assistant thanks to HACS and SonoffLan but it will still be necessary to integrate it into the Ewelink app beforehand. There is, however, one drawback when using this application, which is the fact that you use a Chinese cloud to control the Sonoff range wifi modules.

    Info

    When operating via the Ewelink application, activating a switch will not cause sounds or vibrations only light will be available unlike the 'physical activation.

    When searching for Wifi, a blue light flashes on the top of the module then ends up turning off once connected to the network.

    Info

    The interchangeable covers are not available at domadoo however you can obtain them directly from Itead or on the Aliexpress purchase link

    Characteristics of Sonoff TX Ultimate T5-86

    The switch will delight your eyes and ears, firstly the responsiveness of the keys is sensational and on the entire surface, very responsive, you just need to touch the key for it to it turns on, noise, vibration and led light will activate just that, negative point you will not be able to deactivate these options they are for the moment inseparable.

    View of the switch with the Star shell installed, changing the shell is quite easy

    Second point you can configure the LED display outside of the operation of the switches, and thus illuminate the module permanently. This option offers 8 different themes: (Night light, Party, Leisure, Color, Childhood, Wiper, Fairy, Starburst), you will definitely find lighting to suit you.

    Below are the different LED lighting image themes available for this switch.

    Available Sonoff functions TX Ultimate T5-86

    No less than 6 functions are available with this switch:

    • Set of 8 LED lights
    • Vibes
    • Sounds during physical activation
    • light under the switch activated
    • interchangeable shells
    • Actions Slide Left or Right

    Interchangeable Shells

    Sonoff provides optional interchangeable shells, for the European version you have the choice between cartoon (cat/bear) or Star to spruce up your switch, the American version 120 offers a somewhat different Cartoon in my opinion more successful for a bedroom Boy.

    European cartoon, with its packaging

    Star case with its packaging.

    Additional point, the Sonoff TX Ultimate T5-86 is sold with a white shell but it is possible to interchange it, customization has almost no limits, the European version offers two shells as options Cartoon or Star, However, I regret the American version of the cartoon representing whale and coral, which is not available for the European version, the blue would correspond better to young boys.

    Sonoff TX Ultimate T5-86 and Home Assistant

    The switch is controllable via HomeAssistant thanks to SonoffLAN and HACS integration, I wrote an article about this, read it and you will definitely succeed in the integration.

    Good news The Sonoff TX-Ultimate works at 95% without the Cloud, in Lan mode everything is controllable except slide 1 and 2 In Auto or Cloud mode the Sonoff TX Ultimate T5-86 works at 100%

    In Auto or Cloud mode the left and right slide is recognized, in Lan mode this is not the case for the moment I posted a github issue on this subject

    During the next update of the SonoffLAN plugin you will be able to easily control the themes via home assistant, to carry out these tests I updated the SonoffLAN lights.py file

    Info

    When operating via SonoffLAN and Home Assistant, activating a switch will not cause sounds or vibrations only light will be available unlike the 'physical activation.

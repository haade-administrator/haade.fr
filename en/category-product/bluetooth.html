    Bluetooth

    Bluetooth is a telecommunications standard allowing bidirectional exchange of data over short distances using UHF radio waves in a frequency band between 2.402Ghz and 2.480GHz. Its goal is to simplify connections between nearby electronic devices by eliminating wired connections.

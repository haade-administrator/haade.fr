> The most powerful and versatile smart home hub on the market for its size.

### SMLight SMHUB NANO MG24

The **SMHUB NANO** is much more than just a gateway. It is a full hub running **SMHUB-OS** (an optimized Linux distribution), capable of running Zigbee2MQTT, Home Assistant (via MQTT) or serving as a Matter router.

- **Dual Chip:** Silabs EFR32MG24 (Zigbee/Thread) + ESP32-S3
- **Linux OS:** ARM-based, allowing advanced local management
- **Connectivity:** USB-C, Wi-Fi, and USB Host support (via powered hub)
- **Standalone:** Can operate without an external box for network management
- **Updates:** Simplified web interface for firmware and OS

Ideal for decentralizing your installation or creating an ultra-high-performance and secure Zigbee/Thread network.

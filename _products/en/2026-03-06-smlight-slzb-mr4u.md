> The ultimate adapter for multi-protocol Zigbee and Thread.

### SMLight SLZB-MR4U

The **SLZB-MR4U** is a unique remote coordinator featuring two latest-generation radio chips to manage your Zigbee and Thread networks simultaneously and without compromise.

- **Dual Radio Chip:** Texas Instrument CC2674P10 + Silabs EFR32MG26
- **Multi-interface:** Ethernet (PoE), Wi-Fi, or USB
- **Integrated VPN:** Wireguard client for secure remote use
- **Bluetooth Proxy:** Via the integrated ESP32-S3
- **Compact Format:** Robust aluminum case with 5dBi antennas

It is the perfect tool for those who do not want to choose between Zigbee and Matter/Thread.

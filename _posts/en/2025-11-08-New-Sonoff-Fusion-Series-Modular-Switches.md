Following the creation of the **Shelly wall switch**, compatible with their micromodules, **Sonoff is following suit in this market and creating the Fusion range**, switch boxes compatible with the range's micromodules, closely resembling the **design of Siemens switches** widely available on the market.

> **But Sonoff doesn't stop there** and offers **DIY solutions through 3D printing**.

Below is a screenshot of the Shelly switches, pioneers in this field, especially with the Zigbee protocol. The Shelly modules are well-designed; however, the all-plastic construction **looks a bit cheap**.

## Let's start with the Fusion E1GS/SL range

Let's not waste time on the introduction, as the range expands rapidly.

The **Fusion E1GS** range is designed to integrate the **ZBMINIR2, MINIR4, and MINIR4M** models, which are three mini-switches, each operating under a different protocol: **Zigbee, Wi-Fi, and Matter**. Sonoff thus covers all protocols for integrating switches.

## Wall Plates for 2 or 3 Switches

Don't worry, you can easily buy the switches without the modules, so you can customize them with the 2 or 3 module cover plate. Let's move on to the plates/frames for the Fusion switches in the range. Like other brands, these plates allow you to aesthetically integrate two or three switches.

{% include product-embed.html image="cadre-interrupteur-2-ou-3-bouton-mais-pas-que.png" title="Wall Switch Frame" brand="Sonoff" description="Frame, double or triple switch plate for the Sonoff Fusion range"  domlink="eclairage-connecte/8378-sonoff-cadre-d-interrupteur-mural-sonoff-orb-2-canaux.html" sonofftech="wall-switch-frame-orb-2-3-gang" %}
